Midwest JS

August 16-18 - MINNEAPOLIS, MN

Midwest JS is a premier technology conference focused on the JavaScript ecosystem. The conference has been a tremendous success the past three years and we hope to make it even better in 2017!
The conference will be held in downtown Minneapolis at the University of St. Thomas.

As in prior years there will be social activities for all conference attendees and sponsors in the evenings after the talks are done for the day. The exact details around the social activities are still in the works, check back for more details!
