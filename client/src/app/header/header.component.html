Midwest JS

August 8th-10th, 2018 - MINNEAPOLIS, MN

Midwest JS is a premier technology conference focused on the JavaScript ecosystem. The conference has been a tremendous success the past four years and we hope to make it even better in 2018!
The conference will be held downtown Minneapolis at the University of St. Thomas.

This year will be the 5th annual Midwest JS! As a special thank you to all of the support we've received these past years our happy hour will be held on the rooftop of the Union!
